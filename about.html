About Me

Hello! I'm DJ, a passionate web designer and developer with a background that adds remarkable depth to my journey. My venture into the digital realm originated from a fascination with crafting captivating and interactive websites that offer exceptional user experiences through the enchantment of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript artistry. My coding prowess extends beyond the frontend, encompassing backend technologies like PostgreSQL, PHP, and Python, which empower me to construct dynamic and efficient web applications that seamlessly cater to user needs.

Before my foray into web development, I thrived as an automotive technician for nearly 15 years, a transition marked by both challenges and necessity. As a disabled veteran, my physical limitations rendered skilled technical tasks unfeasible. It was my eldest child's independent coding mastery at the tender age of 13 that ignited my passion for coding and website development, propelling me onto this transformative journey.

Currently, I'm diligently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology with a specific focus on the intricacies of multi-tier architecture. This educational trajectory delves deep into the interplay of various technological layers, illuminating the path to creating potent and scalable applications.

In tandem with my learning odyssey, I'm fervently constructing a portfolio that serves as a testament to my evolving proficiency and creative capabilities. Each project, regardless of its scale, serves as a stepping stone towards embracing grander opportunities. Whether crafting a personal blog, fashioning an e-commerce platform, or pioneering a cutting-edge web application, I relish embracing novel challenges and transmuting them into digital realities.

I wholeheartedly invite you to explore my portfolio and engage with me if you seek collaboration or wish to explore the prospects of your next project. Together, let's conjure something truly remarkable!
