Antique Gallery page

I created this page for a close personal friend that restores vintage furniture. At the time that I made the page, I thought that they were antiques, however, I learned that there is a difference between the two. An antique is an item that is still in relatively good condition, and has its original paint, decals and materials. A vintage item is an item that has been restored to the original condition, by means of replacing paint, materials and decals.
